Vegetable Fried Rice

This vegetable fried rice combines the nutty flavor of brown rice with the fresh taste of bell peppers, baby peas, and other vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 ½ cups quick-cooking brown rice
  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 small green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 teaspoons minced garlic
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 cups frozen petite peas
  • ½ tablespoon and ½ teaspoon sesame oil
  • ¼ cup roasted peanuts

Directions

Step 1

Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Stir in rice. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes. Set aside.

Step 2

Heat peanut oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add onions, bell pepper, garlic, and red pepper flakes. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes.

Step 3

Increase heat to medium-high. Stir in cooked rice, green onions, and soy sauce; cook and stir for 1 minute. Add peas and cook 1 minute more.

Step 4

Remove from heat. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with peanuts.
